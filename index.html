About Unmuted

A serious youth-led platform for voices that deserve more room.

Unmuted empowers underprivileged children through debate, public speaking, and communication-based leadership initiatives. The organization creates platforms where young people can learn to think critically, speak with structure, and use dialogue as a tool for change.

Through beneficiary partners such as Stand N Stride Foundation, Unmuted connects competitive excellence with community impact by turning talented speakers into mentors for students who can benefit from debate, spoken English, aptitude-building education, and leadership training.

The problem and the response

Debate should not belong only to privileged classrooms.

01

The gap

Public speaking and debate opportunities are often limited to students from privileged educational backgrounds. Many talented students lack exposure, mentorship, and safe platforms to develop communication skills.

  • Underprivileged children rarely access debate, spoken English, or leadership training.
  • Talented speakers often have no long-term pathway beyond a competition stage.
  • Most debate events end at rankings, trophies, and announcements.
02

The Unmuted solution

Unmuted identifies strong young speakers through competitions, selects promising participants for the Fellowship Program, and equips them to mentor underprivileged children through workshops and communication sessions.

  • Competitive excellence becomes community impact.
  • Fellows gain internships, mentorship, certification, and stipend-based teaching opportunities.
  • Children gain confidence, critical thinking, spoken English, and debate skills.

Vision

Opinions as instruments of change, not liabilities.

Unmuted aspires to nurture children into individuals who can find common ground through meaningful dialogue, strong arguments, and the courage to participate in the world around them.

Mission

Knowledge grows when given away.

The mission is to build critical thinking, social, scientific, economic, and political awareness while helping children express themselves fearlessly through peer-led mentorship and real-world learning.

Competition format

UNMUTED Knockout Debate

Think. Speak. Rebut. Win.

As part of the UNMUTED journey, participants will take part in a high-energy knockout debate competition designed to test communication, critical thinking, teamwork, confidence, and the ability to respond under pressure.

This is not just about speaking loudly. It is about thinking clearly, building arguments, listening carefully, and responding smartly.

01

Team Format

Participants can register in teams of 2 to 3 members. Individual participants can also register and will be matched with other individual participants or random teams before the competition.

2-3 members Solo entries allowed Team matching
02

Single-Elimination Knockout

Teams advance round by round after winning their match. One loss eliminates the team from the tournament, keeping the format fast-paced, competitive, and focused on the strongest arguments and sharpest rebuttals.

Win to advance One loss out Fast-paced

Preparation per match

10 minutes
6 min

Research with Internet

Teams may research the topic, collect facts, understand perspectives, and prepare their arguments.

4 min

Offline Preparation

Teams structure speeches, divide roles, plan rebuttals, and finalize their argument strategy without internet access.

Debate per match

10 minutes
  1. 3 min

    Team A presents its main arguments.

  2. 3 min

    Team B presents its main arguments.

  3. 1.5 min

    Team A responds to Team B's arguments.

  4. 1.5 min

    Team B responds to Team A's arguments.

  5. 1 min

    Judges announce the winner and the next match begins.
